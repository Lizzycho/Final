ღ About Her:

Liz Cho is a designer who started to learn a design from her 20s. She graduated her university in Korea. She have studied English as a second language and her major was English language and literature. She went abroad to improve her English, but she learnt some design skills in Canada, at the same time. She decided to learn more about design studies.
At first, it was totally different from Canadian fashion to Korean fashion, which made her confused and frustrated. This is because she was about to have her own brand which is Korean style, but she was about to have her store in Toronto where not many people do not know Korean style. However, she learned that no matter what nationalities people have, they can love Korean fashion, too. In other words, it is not a matter with their nationalities, but with their STYLE . Therefore, She started to make her own style which based on Korean fashion. Her first store in Toronto was successful and she had her online store now!
Now, you learned her background. It is time to enjoy her style with your perspective of fashion. Do you like Skirts? Pants? Or Dresses? You can choose whatever you want, how much you want and wherever you are. Enjoy our style and see you again!
